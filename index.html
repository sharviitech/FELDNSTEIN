Our Process

The Gaanuga Method

A centuries-old tradition from the villages of Andhra Pradesh — where strength meets stillness, and time itself presses the oil.

01
Seed Selection & Cleaning
Farmers handpick premium black sesame seeds from their own fields. Seeds are sun-dried and cleaned without any mechanical intervention to ensure purity from source.
02
The Bull Rotation Begins
Bulls walk in slow circles around the Gaanuga — a wooden and stone press. This generates no heat, no metal friction. The seeds are crushed gently, releasing oil over hours.
03
Natural Gravity Filtration
The extracted oil is allowed to settle and naturally filter through cloth — no chemical processing, no bleaching, no deodorisation. The raw oil retains its full spectrum of nutrients.
04
Small-Batch Bottling
Bottled same-day in small batches to lock in freshness. Each bottle is labeled by hand and dispatched directly from Mothadaka village to your kitchen — no middlemen.
