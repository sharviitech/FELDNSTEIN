About Feld 'n' Stein

Where Strength
Meets Stillness

At Feldnstein, our oils are extracted through the timeless bull-rotated Gaanuga — a Stone & Wood Press — where slow, circular stone pressing powered naturally by bull rotation preserves the oil's authentic aroma, texture, and living nutrients in their purest form.

Free from heat and chemicals, every bottle embodies heritage, harmony, and handcrafted excellence. We are bull-driven cold pressed oil makers, made by farmers, for families who value purity, tradition, and wellness.

Why Feldnstein

Six Reasons to
Never Compromise

🐂

Bull Driven Cold Press

No motor, no electricity — only the patient, rhythmic strength of a bull turning the Gaanuga stone at nature's pace.

🌡️

Zero Heat Generation

Unlike machine cold presses that generate moderate friction heat, bull rotation produces absolutely none. Enzymes stay alive.

⚗️

No Metal Contact

Stone and wood only touch the seed. Machine presses use metal components that can leach and oxidise your oil.

🏺

Ancient Wisdom

The Gaanuga method has been unchanged for thousands of years because it simply cannot be improved upon.

🌾

Empowering Farmers

Made by farmers in Mothadaka village, Guntur — every purchase supports traditional livelihoods and rural families.

✨

Rich & Traditional Flavour

Machine oils taste mild and uniform. Gaanuga oil tastes like your grandmother's kitchen — deep, rich, unmistakable.

🔬

Living Nutrients

Sesamin, sesamolin, vitamin E — the micronutrients that heat destroys. In Gaanuga oil, they survive intact.
